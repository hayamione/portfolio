About Me

Hello! Thanks For Visiting My Website

I am Haya Zubair. I am a graduate with computer applications and I am a web designer that creates websites and responsive user interfaces. I am currently pursuing masters in computer applications and my journey for this started when I got into college and learned the fundamentals of web development and about how deep that goes.
After my bachelors, I started as a Web Developer Intern in an IT Company and was able to get hands on experience about how all of that works. I designed many of the landing pages and some websites as well that are made using the latest tools and are SEO and user friendly as well.

That's where I'm at now and I am trying to learn and build as much as I can. I’m looking forward to building more complex projects also that are truly useful in someones everyday life and work.
